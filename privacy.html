P2C Pulse – Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the P2C Pulse iOS app (the “App”) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards personal information.

Relationship to Power to Change Ministries (“P2C”): P2C Pulse is a ministry tool developed and operated for use within Power to Change – Students. This policy is intended to align with P2C’s organization-wide privacy commitments. If there is any inconsistency between this policy and P2C’s official Privacy Policy, P2C’s policy governs on organization-wide matters.

1. Who we are

The App is developed and operated by the P2C Pulse team for Power to Change – Students (“we”, “us”, “our”). For the purposes of this policy, “personal information” means information about an identifiable individual, consistent with applicable privacy laws.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account details: email address and basic profile information you provide when you create an account.
  • In‑app content: habit names, habit status and history, notes and customizations you choose to save.
  • Support communications: messages you send to us for help or feedback.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: interactions with App features (e.g., screens viewed, time spent) to help us maintain and improve the App.
  • Device information: device type, operating system version, and device identifiers necessary for functionality (e.g., push notifications) and diagnostics.

We do not collect payment or donation information through the App. If this ever changes, we will update this policy and provide appropriate notices.

3. How we use information

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Personalize your experience and remember your preferences.
  • Send service-related communications (e.g., account notices, changes, security alerts).
  • Send optional push notifications (you can turn these off in iOS settings).
  • Provide support and respond to inquiries.
  • Perform analytics to improve reliability and performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Marketing & ministry communications: The App does not send marketing emails or newsletters by default. If, in the future, we introduce promotional or ministry-related communications, we will seek your consent where required and provide an easy opt‑out.

4. Legal bases & consent

We rely on one or more of the following legal bases (as applicable): your consent; performance of a contract (providing the App you requested); legitimate interests such as improving the App and ensuring security; and compliance with legal obligations. Where consent is required (for example, enabling push notifications), you may withdraw it at any time through your device settings or by contacting us.

5. When we share information

5.1 Service providers

We use trusted service providers to host and process data on our behalf, including Supabase (application hosting, database, authentication) and other vendors necessary to operate the App. These providers may access personal information solely to provide services to us and are subject to confidentiality and security obligations.

5.2 Within the P2C family

Where appropriate and consistent with P2C’s organization-wide privacy commitments, we may share information with P2C affiliates/ministries for internal administrative purposes, to support ministry operations, or as otherwise described in this policy.

5.3 Legal and safety

We may disclose information if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to: (a) comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request (including subpoenas or warrants); (b) protect the rights, property, or safety of P2C, users, or the public; or (c) enforce our terms and investigate potential violations, fraud, or security issues.

No sale of personal information: We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

6. Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including providing the App, complying with our legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements. We may retain certain information even after a deletion request where we have a lawful basis or operational necessity (e.g., security logs, legal holds).

7. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures intended to protect personal information against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to strengthen our safeguards.

8. Your rights & choices

  • Access / correction: You may request access to or correction of personal information we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated personal information, subject to lawful exceptions (see Retention).
  • Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a portable format where feasible.
  • Restriction / objection: Where applicable, you may ask us to restrict certain processing or object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

Identity verification: To protect your privacy, we may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests. We will respond to requests within a reasonable period and in accordance with applicable law.

9. Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps. If we learn that we have collected such information without consent, we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide appropriate notice (for example, in‑App notice or email) and update the “Last updated” date above. Your continued use of the App after the effective date of an updated policy constitutes acceptance of the changes, where permitted by law.
