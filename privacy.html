Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Sept. 2, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how P2C Pulse ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Email Address: We collect your email address when you create an account to provide our services and communicate with you.
  • Habit Data: We collect and store information about your habits, including habit names, completion status, tracking history, and any notes or customizations you add.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the App, including features accessed and time spent in the App.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our habit tracking services
  • Send you push notifications to remind you about your habits (only if you opt in)
  • Analyze and improve our App's functionality and user experience
  • Communicate with you about your account or our services
  • Respond to your questions, comments, or support requests

How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party backend service provider. Supabase maintains industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your data is stored on Supabase's servers, which may be located in various countries.

Data Sharing and Access

  • App Creators: The creators and operators of this App have access to your data to provide and improve our services.
  • Supabase: Your data is processed and stored by Supabase in accordance with their privacy policy and security standards.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Push Notifications

We may send you push notifications to remind you about your habits. You can control these notifications through your device settings or within the App. You can opt out of notifications at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you wish to delete your account or request that we no longer use your information, please contact us.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of certain uses of your information
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date above.
